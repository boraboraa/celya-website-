What cookies are

Cookies are small files a website can store on your device. Some are strictly necessary to make a site work; others measure audience or serve ads. Under EU/Belgian rules, non-essential cookies require your consent.

What we use today

Celya’s website is intentionally lightweight. We use Google Analytics 4 to measure visits — but only if you accept it on the cookie banner. We set no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. The site also loads web fonts from Google.

How to control cookies

You can block or delete cookies at any time in your browser settings. Most browsers let you refuse third-party cookies or clear stored data. Note that blocking essential items may affect how the site works.
