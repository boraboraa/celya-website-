06Fair questions

Asked & answered honestly.

Is Janet a real person?
No — Janet is Celya's AI agent, and she never pretends otherwise. She introduces herself as an AI on every call and in every message, in the customer's language. That disclosure is required by the EU AI Act, and we build it in rather than bolt it on.
What happens when she doesn't know the answer?
She says so, and hands the conversation to you. Janet only states facts that come from your approved business data — your products, prices and rules. When something falls outside that, she doesn't improvise: she takes a message, flags it for a human, and tells the customer when to expect an answer.
Which languages does she speak?
French, Dutch and English — spoken and written. She detects the customer's language and answers in it.
Which channels does she cover?
Phone (she answers calls with a natural voice), WhatsApp and email. Every conversation, on every channel, lands in one log you can read.
Do I stay in control of what she sends?
Yes — you choose her autonomy level. Start with Janet drafting every reply for your approval. Let her handle routine replies on her own when you're comfortable. Move to full autopilot when she's earned it. You can change level, or step into any conversation, at any time — and every action she takes is logged and reversible.
Is this GDPR-compliant?
Yes. Your data is hosted in the EU (Frankfurt), Celya acts as data processor under a signed DPA, outbound contact respects consent, and opt-outs are honored automatically.
What does it cost?
There's no price list — every business gets a scoped proposal with one clear price and no surprises. It starts with a free 20-minute call: we audit how leads reach you today, then propose exactly what you need. Start small, scale when it works.
